What we do

Manufacturing-grade FDM parts with predictable quality, informed by DFM and rigorous QA.

Precision FDM Manufacturing

Dimensional accuracy and surface finish you can rely on — layer heights down to 0.06 mm, calibrated machines, verified materials.

DFM Review & Consultation

We analyse your CAD for strength, printability and cost efficiency, then propose targeted changes to meet your requirements.

Finishing & Assembly

Post-processing options including sanding, vapor smoothing for ABS, inserts, tapping, dye, and sub-assembly — ready for service.

Short-Run Production

Repeatable performance for fixtures, enclosures and jigs. Batch QC, labeling and packaging included as needed.

FAQ

What file formats do you accept?

STL, 3MF for printing; STEP/IGES if you want DFM review. Include units and preferred material.

How fast can you print?

Most jobs are ready within 24–72 hours depending on size, quantity and finish. Larger batches may take longer.

Do you ship?

Yes — local pickup in Sydney or we can arrange courier delivery.

Do you offer resin or SLS?

We focus on FDM/FFF in-house. For specialty processes we can advise options or arrange through partners on request.
